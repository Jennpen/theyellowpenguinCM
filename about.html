About Us!

In 2019, the first ever albino penguin with yellow fur was discovered in South Georgia, a remote island in the Atlantic. When Jennifer Rabideau, the founder of "The Yellow Penguin '', first discovered this unique bird she knew right then and there that it would be her mascot for her lifelong dream of opening up a coffee shop. So, on July 7, 2024 her dream came true and The Yellow Penguin was born. Since then it has been our mission to serve the best quality coffee to our loyal penguin lovers. We strive to be as unique as this bird and we hope that our coffee will leave a lasting impact on our community.
